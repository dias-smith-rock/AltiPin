Features

Six core modules

From orientation to GPX archives, AltiPin brings essential outdoor data into one dark OLED interface — clear, power-efficient, and easy to read.

🧭

Compass

Digital compass, bearing, coordinates, and live weather for quick orientation outdoors.

⛰️

Altitude

Barometric elevation, indoor/outdoor detection, floor inference, magnetic field, and weather data.

🏎️

Speedometer

Analog gauge with driving, cycling, running, and walking modes plus session stats.

🏃

Activity

Track duration, distance, speed, and elevation gain; face-to-face team location sharing.

📷

Geo Camera

Stamp photos and videos with elevation, coordinates, and time. Stored locally on device.

🗺️

Track History

Auto-generate standard GPX 1.1 files with merge, export, and history review.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device

AltiPin requires no account. Tracks, photos, and elevation history are stored locally on your iPhone by default — not uploaded to our servers.

Local GPX storage Tracks are saved as standard .gpx files in the app sandbox. Export anytime.
No account required Use all core features without signing up or logging in.
Ephemeral team relay Face-to-face teams relay location via Realtime — not persisted on the server.
Transparent permissions Location, camera, microphone, and photo library are requested only when needed.
